Who's behind

MusicBRICK born in Shanghai, from a Sino-Italian group of students at Tongji university. Its aim is to give intuitive, easy and versatile software that overcome the inaccessibility involving beginners with today’s products. With its namesake product it wants to encourage everyone to let themselves be surrounded by the beauty and charm of music.
